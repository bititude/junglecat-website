Qualification & Courses Management

An academy may provide a plethora of courses under different qualifications, on Jungle Cat the Administrators can easily organize and manage different courses and its respective details with ease. It helps in listing and arrangement of courses under qualification levels. Each of these course listings can further be expanded and shared with teachers and students, as it may include syllabuses, academic calendars etc. This makes it easier for the teachers and the students for quick and easy sync.

Class Room Experience with Unit Wise Feed

The class room experience of the new era is here, The Unit wise Feed feature of Jungle Cat provides a state-of-the-art virtual class room experience. With each unit/subject having a feed that provides adequate space for sharing information in any medium with the respective teacher and classmates, allowing more space for discussion, collaboration and more importantly the feeling of companionship of the old classrooms.

Recorded video sessions

With the virtual way of learning, recording certain sessions become necessary, sometimes for the teacher to evaluate or for the absent students to watch. On Jungle Cat, video classes can be recorded and can be availed from the video library, where it will be perfectly arranged and be available to watch on requirement. Recording sessions and archiving them properly has never been easier.

Time and Schedule Management

Jungle Cat solves the huge task of time management with the schedule system synced across various participants. A teacher can view their schedules; video sessions, meetings, deadlines of various tasks they have assigned, meanwhile a student can view relevant information by the teachers on their calendars, like video classes, assignment deadlines etc. An administrator can over watch these sessions and schedules, allowing them to intervene when necessary. This synced form of calendar and clock allows everyone to be on the same page, increasing productivity and decreasing unnecessary clutters and time wastage.

Attendance management

The days of roll calling and having to manage the attendance are long gone, as the teachers and the students are integrated into the Jungle Cat ecosystem, attendance taking and management becomes automatic based on who showed up for the video sessions and meetings. This data is stored and can be analysed and retrieved when required in a jiffy. This data is also synced with the profiles of the students and will be available to the teachers and admins.

Assessment management with automated audit evidence system

Assessing the student can needlessly be tiring when using a handful of programmes and platforms, issues of uploading, evaluating and result publishing is hectic if not properly done. With Jungle Cat's efficient Assessment management system and Automated Audit System, Teachers can assign students with various tasks and students can upload their submissions in any formats required, teachers can directly evaluate inside the platform itself and mark them accordingly, what makes Jungle Cat's system better than most is how both teachers and students are notified of each step in the process, adding to accountability on all parts. The platform also provides assessment reports for the teachers with necessary summaries and analytics.

Student Portfolio

Jungle Cat provides separate profiles for student users. This lets the student showcase their personal details, achievements and other works they wish to display to other users of the app. They can edit and maintain their profiles whenever necessary. The feature allows the audience of the application to take glance of the student from the profile they have presented to the world.

Teacher profile

Get to know a teacher at a glance. Teacher profiles are a way to know the teacher handling each unit. Both students and admins can overview teacher profiles which display basic details, portfolios, schedule, unit, and other information you may need to know. For a new student, the feature is very helpful. They can check who all are teaching them and take a glance at each teacher's profile before joining the class. The feature assist admins to familiarize themselves with the staff and unit allotted to them. The schedule displayed on the profile is reliable for the teacher, just before the class time. .

Public & Class specific library

As Learning has become online, so has the resources and the books, sharing them has been no fun, with PDFs floating around all our phones and PCs, it's especially necessary to have an organised and easy to access library. Jungle Cat thus provides two options for libraries: A public library where resources on all relevant topics are uploaded and organized quick and easy and A class library which is more of a specific book shelf with access limited to the teacher and students of a specific class. Organizing the resources in this way reduces clutter and head ache in the long run.

TAS Automation for RTOs in Australia

Australian RTO's follow rules and regulations, accordingly to which organizations have to run. They constantly carry out TAS (Training and assessment strategy) based on performance criteria, performance evidence, and performance knowledge to map organizations. In such cases, Jungle case provides TAS Automation to the RTOs in Australia to check compliance of the organizations. Increased transparency of the feature boosts organizations to exhibit their best. Jungle Cat Makes Compliance Systemic, Ubiquitous and Unobtrusive.
